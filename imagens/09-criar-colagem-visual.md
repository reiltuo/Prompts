# Criar colagem visual

**Categoria:** Imagens

**O que faz:** Gera uma colagem de referências para manter consistência de pessoa, roupa, ambiente e estilo.

**TXT para copiar:** [abrir TXT](txt/09-criar-colagem-visual.txt)

## Prompt

```text
Use the woman from Reference Image 1 as the subject, preserving her face, facial proportions, skin tone, hair,
makeup, and overall identity exactly as shown. Do not alter her appearance, age, facial structure, or hair
features in any way.
Recreate her in the same pose of the 2nd image, camera angle, framing, lighting style, ambient mood, and
surroundings as shown in Reference Image 2. Match the environment closely: similar indoor setting, similar
background feel, same warm/dim cinematic lighting, same depth of field, and same atmosphere.
If clothing is visible in Reference Image 2, apply a similar, not exactly the same, clothing style and colors to
the woman from Reference Image 1 only if it fits naturally, while keeping her body proportions and
appearance unchanged.
The final image should look like:
The exact woman from Reference 1
Placed into the scene, pose, and ambiance of Reference 2
Natural integration, realistic lighting interaction
High realism, detailed skin texture, natural shadows
No face swapping artifacts, no identity blending
Ensure facial consistency at 100%, no morphing between references.
no emojis, no text.
```
