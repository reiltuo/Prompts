# Criar colagem facial

**Categoria:** Imagens

**O que faz:** Cria um conjunto de imagens mantendo a identidade facial travada em diferentes composições.

**TXT para copiar:** [abrir TXT](txt/08-criar-colagem-facial.txt)

## Prompt

```text
Create multiple ultra-realistic facial images intended for a high-fidelity facial collage, capturing the EXACT
SAME PERSON with extreme precision.
NON-NEGOTIABLE FACIAL IDENTITY LOCK
The face must remain 100% identical across all images. Preserve every micro-detail:
Eyes: Identical eye shape, size, spacing, symmetry, eyelid structure, iris color, limbal ring, sclera tone, gaze
depth.
Nose: Identical bridge width and height, nose length and projection, nostril size, shape, flare, spacing, tip
definition and angle.
Mouth & Lips: Identical lip volume (upper and lower separately), shape, curvature, cupid's bow, corners,
mouth width, natural lip texture and color.
Cheeks & Cheekbones: Identical cheekbone placement, cheek volume, nasolabial folds.
Jaw, Chin & Face Shape: Identical jawline width, angle, chin shape, height, projection, overall facial
proportions.
Skin & Micro-Details: Identical skin tone and undertone, visible pores, freckles, moles, subtle marks. No
smoothing, no plastic skin, no AI beauty filters.
Hair: Identical hairline, eyebrow shape, thickness, spacing.
COLLAGE OBJECTIVE
Allowed variations ONLY
Very small camera angle changes (frontal, subtle 3/4
Slight camera distance changes (close-up, tight close-up)
Lighting changes that do NOT alter skin tone
Minimal, non-distracting background changes
STRICTLY AVOID
Any facial drift or reinterpretation
Changing proportions, volume, or symmetry
Beauty filters or makeup that alters facial structure
Cartoon, fantasy, painterly, or AI-art styles
Output only the final image descriptions/prompts. Language: English only.
```
