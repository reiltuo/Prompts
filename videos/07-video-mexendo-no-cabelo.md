**TXT para copiar:** [abrir TXT](txt/07-video-mexendo-no-cabelo.txt)

* The subject starts at a natural distance from the camera

* The gesture is delicate, subtle and realistic, like naturally showing the texture, fit or front detail of the clothing
* She must only interact with clothing elements that already exist in the original image
* Do NOT create or add any bow, lace, ribbon, tie, cord, knot, string, accessory, decoration or new clothing detail

* The movement feels spontaneous and casual
* After briefly touching the fabric, she naturally releases it
* She takes a small step back to her original position
* Smooth transition into the rest of the animation

GENERAL MOTION STYLE:

* Natural, confident, fluid movements
* Not static
* Not exaggerated
* Not robotic
* Real human timing

BODY MOVEMENTS:

* One hand naturally rests on the waist

* One foot forward, the other relaxed behind
* Natural posture
* Relaxed shoulders

CLOTHING PRESENTATION:

* Small, natural movements to highlight the outfit

* No aggressive posing
* No fashion runway exaggeration
* Movements feel spontaneous and casual
* Do not invent new clothing features
* Do not modify the original clothing design
* Preserve the exact outfit from the source image

ADDITIONAL NATURAL GESTURE, VERY SUBTLE:

* After the main body movement sequence, the subject briefly raises one hand
* She lightly brushes her hair backward near the side of the head
* The gesture lasts approximately one second
* The motion is casual and unintentional, like a natural self-adjustment
* No emphasis
* No posing
* The hand immediately returns to a relaxed neutral position

* Neutral, relaxed expression
* Lips softly closed
* Calm, confident look

* A subtle smile appears briefly

* Slight cheek movement only

After releasing the fabric:

* Expression returns to neutral
* Relaxed face
* Natural breathing

A hyper-realistic POV fashion video where the subject feels alive and human, with natural facial expression changes, occasional soft smiles, neutral moments in between, subtle head turns, and realistic clothing interaction without altering or adding any new clothing detail.
